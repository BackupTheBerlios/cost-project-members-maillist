Dear all,

The final Standard Data Exchange Format, taking into account the 
modifications demanded by the 2nd expert meeting + amendments to make it 
fully compatible with the new DCR is now available on the COST website 
(http://wwz.ifremer.fr/cost).

The principal impact is for COSTcore (importation and check 
procedures).  The dbe and mbe developers should not be impacted by this 
new DEF, for simple cases. The more advanced developments like taking 
into account sexes and different species in a box are impacted, but I am 
not sure we are that far in development!

Anyhow, the best way to evaluate this is to implement it. It is 
important for those labs wishing to export their data into the COST 
environment, and my main message in every international forum I 
participate is to anticipate the release of the COST methods by 
exporting their data into the agreed format which is publicly available now.

Best regards

Joel
