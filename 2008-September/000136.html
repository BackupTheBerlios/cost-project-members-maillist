Hi all,

Returning to something we discussed at the meeting. In our market sampling data, biological samples are collected in two ways:

1) as a subsample of a length sample, e.g. measure 200 fish, age 50 of them
2) without a length sample, e.g. select 50 fish, stratified by length group, for ageing from a known (usually) vessel.

In case 2) the 50 fish go in the CA table but which of the other tables need filling, is it only TR? 

This mix of 1) TR-HH-SL-HL-CA with 2) TR-CA is something for people developing functions to look out for.
It also means that for these data the Trip number field will be consistent across tables as it represents a landing but the Station number field will not be. Station number will be length sample number in the HH,SL,HL fields and the age sample number in the CA field.

Regards, David
