Dear colleagues,

there will be a workshop very soon where the COST developments could be 
helpful :

*Workshop on Methods to evaluate and estimate the accuracy of fisheries 
data used for assessment* [WKACCU]
(Co-Chairs: Michael Pennington* and Jon Helge Vølstad*, IMR, Norway 
(will be established and will meet in Bergen, Norway, 27--30 October 
2008 to

   1.

      review the sources of bias and establish general parameters
      (indicators)/procedures to assess the bias on national level of
      fishery statistics (quantities landed, discards, fishing effort,
      CPUE) using available data, and advice on best practices.

   2.

      review the sources of bias and establish general parameters
      (indicators)/procedures to assess the bias on national level of
      biological data collected from the fisheries by investigating
      (both visual and quantitative) the data coverage by stock, area,
      season and fleet


In that view, I would like to prepare a "COST" Working Document 
explaining what we have developed in relation to the estimates of bias, 
i.e. all graphs in EDA package. It would be relevant also to intiate a 
discussion, both for the COST final report and for the WKACU, on the 
bias : definition, measure and understandings of the graphical outcomes.

I demand Alastair and Mathieu to prepare a document showing all graphs 
available in the EDA package. I will launch a discussion later on the 
points cited above. In the meantime, don't hesitate to comment and make 
suggestions on this issue.

Best regards

Joel
