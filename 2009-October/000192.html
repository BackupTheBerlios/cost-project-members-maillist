Hi Valerio 

Yes at the moment only 27.3.a , b , c, and d are supported for area
plots in the Baltic. 

Alternatively you can plot by ICES rects. 

Adding the 21 to 31 areas will have to wait for any additional funding. 

 

If you really wanted to you could make up most of the 21:31 areas from
blocks of ICES rects e.g. 

 

rects <-c(paste(41:37,"G8",sep=""),paste(41:37,"G9",sep=""),

paste(41:38,"H0",sep=""),paste(41:38,"H1",sep=""))

 

vals <-rep(300,length(rects))

 

spacePlot(vals,rects,sum,xlim=c(5,30),ylim=c(50,70),landmass=T,col.land=
"snow2")

 

 

All the best 

Alastair
