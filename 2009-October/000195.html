Hi Valerio,

did you read the supporting publication on this issue 
(http://www.ifremer.fr/docelec/doc/2007/publication-2731.pdf)? You will 
find several study cases and most relevant for you the fact that you can 
use this statistic at different levels (all strata & all length classes 
--> one stratum & one length class). By defaults, you are using it with 
all strata and all length classes, which is relevant to detect outliers 
and misallocation, but you may chose other options. In particular, to 
detect the similarities between 2 metiers, you may focus only on these 2 
metiers, etc... For doing this in COST, you have to create a subset of 
your data and rerun the delta method. It is true that this is not 
specified in the User Manual.

Best regards

Joel
