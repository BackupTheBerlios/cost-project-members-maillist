We can simulate from our model. In fact we always do this as a check on 
the programming. We can simulate pretty much anything you like - any 
number of hauls/journeys with whatever number of landing/discard samples 
you like. We can make the age and length-given-age distributions change 
from stratum to stratum etc. I had assumed this was what we would do for 
COST, because I don't know of any way of simulating data without a 
model, and there isn't any other model. I would be interested to see 
what anyone else has to suggest though. The disadvantage of simulating 
from our model of course is that it gives our estimation method a 
natural advantage (since our model is by definition correct in the 
simulations). I don't see any way around this though.
