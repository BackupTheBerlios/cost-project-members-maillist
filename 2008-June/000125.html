Dear all,

prior to the meeting and summarising the latest discussions in the mailing list, in the forum and in multilateral correspondance, here are the issues to be discussed regarding the COST-FF Data Exchange Format :

* the major discussion is about the sex field in hl table. The issue is about extending this concept to other possibilities than F/M and also extending the concept to take into consideration the taxa in a sampled box. 
The system today is that a given hh$staNum or hh$trpCode is documented with sl$spp related to cl$spp and ce$spp at the population level. for this sl$spp we may have different sexes (case of Nephrops) registered in hl$sex. 
The first problem is that we don't have the sampled weight per sex. It's not a problem to estimate the total number and the total length structure per sex, but how do we calculate the total weight per sex?
The second point is about burried Nephrops. I must say I don't see where is the problem here. The field is optional for unsexed and, unless I miss something, you cannot sex the burried Nephrops? Do we open this field to other occurences? B(urried), ...
