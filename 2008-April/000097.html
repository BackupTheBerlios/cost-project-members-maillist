Dorleta Garcia wrote :

 

Hi Joel,

 

The meeting will be held in Azti-Sukarrieta. The surroundings of 
Sukarrieta are very touristic in summer time and if the weather is good 
the place is really nice.

 

For the accommodation, the closest place, 2 km, is Mundaka which is 
connected to Sukarrieta by train and bus every 30 minutes. Another 
option is to stay in Bilbao, there are buses and trains every 30 minutes 
form Sukarrieta to Bilbao but it takes 1 hour more or less.

 

The closest airport is the Bilbao airport (Loiu), 34km far way from 
Sukarrieta.  The easiest way to get to Sukarrieta from the airport is to 
get a taxi which costs 45€ approximately. The other option is to take a 
bus to Bilbao, and once in Bilbao take a train or bus to Sukarrieta, 
this option is cheaper, 6€ approximately, but it is somewhat uncomfortable.

 

I attach some documents with hotel information. As I said above, June is 
touristic time here, so I recommend booking the hotels as soon as 
possible.  

 

Best regards,

 

Dorleta
