Dear COST members,

The COST project has officially come to an end on the 31st of July 2009.

You can now find

   1. the latest version of all packages on
      http://wwz.ifremer.fr/cost/software_packages
   2. the Data Exchange Format which has been published as a ICES CRR.
      Should be here http://www.ices.dk/products/cooperative.asp in a while.
   3. the final deliverables on http://wwz.ifremer.fr/cost/documentation
      (the CRR is in annex of the Final Report)
   4. and some examples of use for different situations on
      http://wwz.ifremer.fr/cost/documentation/examples

Normally I should feel some relief, which is true, but my feeling is 
rather that only one part of the story has been achieved and there is 
much more to do, both in terms of development/debugging and in terms of 
training...

A great thanks to all of you, and a special thanks to the package 
leaders who have done tremendous work. A very special thank to Mathieu  
who left us last Friday, he is probably the only one knowing (almost) 
every single lines of code of all packages. He will come back in Ifremer 
(Brest) for another contract with the economists in September and will 
continue being involved part-time on COST.

We'll think of proposing a corrective maintenance of COST to the 
Commission, probably channelled through the forthcoming RCMs 
(September). I give rendez-vous to some of you in Bergen for the 
WKPRECISE, and in Berlin for the ASC theme session on precision, and 
eventually in Nantes in April 2010 for the COST training session.

I wish you get all accustomed to the use of COST libraries. I have only 
one recommendation to all of you : USE COST.

My best wishes.

Joel
