Hi,

No variance calculation of that sort in COST, it will complexify too 
much for a result probably marginal compared to the (im)precision we 
have on the catch figures.

The point is that we have to ascertain that information in SL match 
information in CL and that information in CL can be merged between 
countries.

As we cannot add gutted and whole fish together, we have two options
1 - add a field PRESENTATION as suggested in the WIKI. The problem is 
that we need a reference table to combine datasets with different 
presentations
2 - request that all information be transformed previously as whole fish 
by the national labs. The problem arises when gutted fish are sampled!!

My choice went to 1, I can live with 2 or better if someone has another 
solution, but we need to agree on a clear rule.
