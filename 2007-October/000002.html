Hello all. 

I would just like to clarify COST's approach to survey data.

In the COST proposal (pages 7-8) we specify we are estimating parameters
in 
	Module E - discards volume estimates
 	Module H - length and age structure of the catches/landings
	Module I - other biological parameters

Since FRS only collects data for other biological parameters from
research surveys rather than market/discard sampling (I think, apart
from Nephrops which are sexed) I previously assumed that COST also
covered research survey trawl data. 

However, Joel's comment on the data exchange forum 26/09/07 implies
otherwise:

Concerning the survey data, they may be stored in a FF format but no
methods addressing this issue will be developed within COST. There are
different problems and different expertise to search here, but in Lisbon
we can begin the discussion on the prospect of COST II. 	

However, Joel's email "[Cost-project-members] FF5_DEF structure" on the
members-mailing-list 10/10/07 refers to acoustic data. I presume this
was a reference to FF5 formats in general rather than their specific
application to COST - I wasn't aware we were going to consider acoustic
data?
Am I right in thinking that COST does not apply to survey data? Closer
reading of the definition of Module I other biological parameters in EU
Reg 1639/2001 does not help (me) clarify this at all!
I'll be very happy to discover that I've misunderstood, as it reduces
the work load I thought we had to cover in COST. However, due to my
misunderstanding, the text I drafted for the EDA package (WP3) includes
specific mention of research survey data for other biological
parameters.
How do we resolve this if we are not analysing survey data?
all the best
Liz
