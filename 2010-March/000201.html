dear COST members,

the WKCOST will be held very soon now (13-16 April in Nantes). For 
information, the meeting will start on Tuesday 9:00 and end on Friday 
17:00 but the Friday afternoon will be a working session. Due to a heavy 
agenda, I'm only back at the lab from today and I'm preparing the agenda 
and the practicalities for the meeting.

My plan is to authorise any people wishing to attend the meeting, but 
only one computer per country/lab will be authorised to be plugged on to 
internet and carry out practical work and analysis. The reason is that 
it will be extremely inefficient to debug too many computers.

If you're aware of people in your lab attending the meeting, please let 
me know, as they need to be registered on the COST ICES sharepoint (you 
need a login/passwd to see it), and I also need to know how many 
participants there will be.

The package leaders and developers who wish to come and give a hand on 
describing and helping people with the methods they have developed, are 
very much welcome to the meeting. I must remind you that only one 
participant per country will be funded through the DCF.

I understand that David Hirst cannot allocate the budget to the EC DCF 
and COST has no budget left anymore (it's difficult enough to proceed to 
the final payment!), so I have no solution for you David. If you don't 
come, I will just fly over the Bayesian model, if you can come, I will 
give you some time in the agenda to explain the potentialities and 
develop some examples.

Dorleta, can you give a presentation of your package?

I plan to deliver a provisional agenda on the COST sharepoint by the end 
of the afternoon.

Thank you for your attention
