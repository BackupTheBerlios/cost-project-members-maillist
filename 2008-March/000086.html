Dear Joel,
 
Thank you for submitting the interim report for the COST study (Lot 2 of call 2006/15). The report is comprehensive and useful and hence the release of the second instalment of the Commission's contribution to the project can be ordered following the reception of the corresponding invoice.
 
Given the close links that the COST study has with the quality aspects in the framework of the DCR, it is suggested that your interim report is further presented in relevant SGRN meetings and further discussed in the framework of the PGCCDBS activities, in particular in the forthcoming WKPRECISE. 
 
Moreover, I would like to inform you that in the Workprogramme for 2008 of the Administrative Arrangement between DG MARE and JRC there are provisions concerning COST. In particular under the issue of quality development, it is stated that JRC will explore oportunities and technical feasibility of adapting the tools developed by COST to assess the data collected by MS in terms of its accuracy and precision. Consequently, you are kindly requested to keep JRC informed of the progress achieved by COST.
 
You are kindly requested to provide ICES and JRC with a copy if this interim report.
